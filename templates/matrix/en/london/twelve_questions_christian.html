Christian Graham

Profile and Leo's Take
Age：23　Years in London: 23

Christian was introduced to me through my sister Maya. Christian, an aspiring actor an playwright, showed me around Brick Lane and one of his favourite cafes “Vintage Emporium”. (The place was filled with antique furniture and blasting honky-tonk from an old gramophone; literally one of the coolest cafes I’d ever been to.) It was also fun talking about what its like to be in the theater scene in London, and I learned a lot, from all the indie productions to writing workshops. Plainly put, Christian was just one of those guys you get excited about when thinking about the future. He also gave numerous unique answers, with my favourites being the representations (Q.3), his images (Q.5) where I encountered a first, wake/sleep (Q.10), and his moments (Q.11). So Christian, do make it over to Japan one day, and me and Maya will show you around this time :)
