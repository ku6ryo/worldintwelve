Profile and Leo's Take
Age：24　Years in London: 19

Dan was introduced to me by Kelly. Dan is a photographer and art director, and one of those figures you just want to become friends with—he is insanely talented, hard working, has high aspirations, nice, cool, and fun to be with. I mean, holy hell. I met Dan a few times while in London, and not only did he take time to meet me on his only day off in a few weeks working a double shift, he introduced me to other cool friends (who’ll come up in later 12 Qs :) ), I got to go camera shopping with him (he got his awesome FujiFilm X-T1 I think it was) and the next time we met he even made me burgers at his place. I mean, HOLY HELL!! And just look at all the shows and artists he’s shot…and what he’s overcome to get here…and the mountains he’s climbed, both figuratively and literally… Two words. Mad Respect. That’s all I have. And of course, his answers are great too. They’re all good so its hard to choose recommendations, but if I had to, I’d say his place and thing (Q.3 ), the future (Q.6), marriage and children (Q.9), and his moments (Q.11). Anyhow, Dan, I know I’ll be seeing you again, and come to Japan when you’re free!! Although I know “free” is tough haha.
