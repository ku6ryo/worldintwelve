India Bourne

Profile and Leo's Take
Age：29　Years in London: 6

India was introduced to me through Kelly, and meeting India was one of those funky cases (and incredibly lucky) where I was encountering a person I had known of previously. Back in Japan, I was asked to create a parody of an idol group’s pop song by one of my friends at NHK for a radio show. At that time, a different friend showed me a clip by Ben Howard doing a cover of “Call Me Maybe“ and I though it was one of the greatest things ever, eventually using it as inspiration for my own parody. Check out the clip. Yes, India is the cellist for (and childhood friend of) Ben Howard. How freaking awesome. So when Kelly (who is friends with many a musician on both sides of the Atlantic) said she could introduce me to India, it was as if some Karma God magic was being performed right before my eyes.

Anyway, so India was kind enough to take time out of her busy schedule before going on tour, and as I imagined, not only was she super nice (the day we met was bloody stormy), she gave some amazing answers. Actually, her goal (Q.12) might be the coolest answer yet! I also like her London aspects (Q.3), images (Q.5), and her moments (Q.11). So thanks again India, and I hope you guys will come to Japan to play one day!! (If not, I’ll just have to go to one of your shows elsewhere :) )

India's own music is still in production and not out yet...so I guess you could listen to Ben Howard? (Not that he really needs an introduction lol)
