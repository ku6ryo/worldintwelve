Lena Ackermann & Hagen Brügmann

Profile and Leo's Take
[Age] Lena: 26, Hagen: 30　[Years in Berlin] Both around 1

Lena and Hagen were introduced to me through Anni. I had an evening meal with Lena and Hagen in one of their favourite restaurants, and this was cool because it was one of the few occasions where I was invited to a location west of Mitte, where I was staying. East Berlin and West Berlin have their obvious differences, and I could feel this in just the atmosphere and vibes of the residents. It was also fun talking with Lena and Hagen because it was right before they were about to have their baby, and this had an impact on some of the answers they gave. [Note: When talking with Lena post giving-birth, she said “A lot of the answers would be different now” which I thought was very interesting] This whole project was about meeting people at various stages in their life, and this one was one of the most significant. Both Lena and Hagen gave many great answers (idk, is it a Berlin thing to give thoughtful answers? lol), but if I were to choose a few, they would be the month (Q.1), the partner (Q.8), marriage/children (Q.9), and dreams (Q.12). So Lena and Hagen, it was such a pleasure talking with you, and I’d love to meet again, maybe when the little one is a little bit older…?? Haha, cheers!!
