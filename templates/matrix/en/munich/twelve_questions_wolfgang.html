Wolfgang Gollwitzer

Profile and Leo's Take
Age：41　Years in Paris: 20

Wolfgang was introduced to me by Mitsuhisa. Wolfgang is an architect in Munich, and he was kind enough to invite me to his studio. It was the first time I had ever visited an architect studio, and as a fan of architecture and interior design, it was an amazing experience. I was able to see the models, and some of the interns showed me first hand how they create designs on facades and ceilings. I also had a beer with Wolfgang and his employees during the post-workday meeting/debriefing session, where I heard various stories from the interns, some of them coming from as far as Japan and Nigeria. Wolfgang himself answered the questions with simplicity and wit—it became more of a conversation than an interview, and that was fun. The positive energy and confidence he held I admired greatly, and was something I felt I could learn from. For his answers, although I recommend all of them, I would choose the future (Q.6), the children (Q.9), the moments (Q.11—I am so jealous of his happy moment!!!), and his dreams (Q.12). So Wolfgang thanks so much for taking time out of your busy schedule, and maybe next time it’ll be Oktoberfest!!

Check out Wolfgang's work here!! www.studioga.de.
