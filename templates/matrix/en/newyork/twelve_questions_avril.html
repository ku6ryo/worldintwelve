Avril Koblitz

Proflie: Age：21　Years in New York： 7 1/2 months


Leo's Take
Avril is actually my second cousin. Avril is one of those people whom I guess we in society call “third culture”; although her parents are American and Japanese, she grew up in Germany and China, and spent years abroad in Chile and London. Wow. And this background of hers is what makes her way of thinking so unique. (You can see this in say, Q.1, where no one before or after has answered in her way.) It’s truly fascinating talking with someone who has an equidistant relationship and feel with so many different places and cultures, especially in this era where extremities come out on top and everyone needs their own specific, concrete niche. All of Avril’s answers are thought-provoking, especially at the metalevel when you realize how much the concept of “identity” resides in each one, but if I had to choose, I would recommend her month (Q.1), her country (Q.2), her flag (Q.4), and her dreams (Q.12). So thanks again Avril, we’ll meet again, and maybe makes some good Japanese food together!!
