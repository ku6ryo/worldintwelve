Holly Murphy & Alex Oka

Profile and Leo's Take
[Holly] Age：26　Years in New York： About 3 years
[Alex] Age：26　Years in New York： About 2 years

Holly and Alex were introduced to me through my childhood friend Shaun. These two are basically what you envision a 20 something NYC couple from the west coast (Portland to be exact--REPRESENT) to be: laid back, chill, easy going, fun, visionary, open-minded, and most importatntly, following their passions and living the life. Holly has an amazing career as a interior designer, and also designed her own home, which was definitely one of the coolest pads I've visited. Alex is a talented music producer and track maker, and I have been a fan of his melodious yet heavy tracks ever since he was in university. Definitely check out their work below. I spent a wonderful time with the two of them, talking about life while nibbling on crackers, and I even went to a Blazers game with Alex later in Brooklyn (let me just say we will never see those Blazers again...as I weep in my pillow). Anyway, enought with tangets...so for the questoins, it was the first time I had done two people at once, and quite frankly it was a lot of fun. There is obviously much more of a conversation feel to them, and I think that can be seen when reading the text too. I recommend the representations (Q.3), the flag (Q.4), wake up / go to bed (Q.10), and the moments (Q.11). So Holly and Alex, I know for a fact I'll be seeing you guys in the spring next year...man I am excited already!!!

→Check out Holly's work at: www.studioholly.com and Alex and his label at: peote.nyc
