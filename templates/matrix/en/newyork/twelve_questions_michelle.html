Michelle Weiss

Profile and Leo's Take
Age：28　Years in New York： Almost 10

Michelle was introduced to me through Sayaka, and she showed me around an area of New York that one native New Yorker I talked to didn't know existed lol. The complex is called Tudor City in Midtown East, adjacent to the United Nations, and it has some of the most architecturally interesting apartments in Manhattan. It was so fun talking with Michelle before and after the 12 Questions, and she gave me insight and knowledge on so many topics ranging from 20-somethings immigrating to urban areas to the rise of land prices in North Dakota. We also went to a bodega after drinks, and gained insight on another important factor useful while living in New York--the sandwiches you get at these places are the best bang for your buck in town! Haha. Regarding the questions Michelle gave a lot of unique answers, but my recommendations are her month (Q.1), her images (Q.5), her children (Q.9), and her dream (Q.12). Thanks again Michelle it was great to meet up, and do come to Japan when Sayaka and I are there!!

Q11

Let’s say… I don’t know if this is the absolute happiest moment.

We have a lot of happy moments, I mean I wish that upon everybody, but I was actually real excited to get into college. It was a really pivotal moment for me. You know in high school, I got good grades, but I was also one of those students who had to try really really hard. I couldn’t coast through a class or take an exam without studying… I had to study really hard and then maybe I did well, but it paid off because I got into college and I was… I just remember seeing the couple acceptance letters I got, because I only applied to a few schools. I already had it part way figured out and this just kind of helped me push myself in the direction I wanted to go in. So, yeah.

>>> Do you have a terrifying moment?

Yeah…so a close family member of mine passed away… when I was very young, when I was pretty young, when I was 12.
