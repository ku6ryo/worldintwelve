Ezgi Samioglu & Baris Ekinci

Profile and Leo's Take
[Age] Ezgi: 29, Baris: 34　[Years in Paris] Ezgi: 6, Baris: 9

Ezgi and Baris were introduced me through Shu. Ezgi and Baris are a Turkish couple, and I had an amazing time with them drinking and eating in one of the more niche areas of Paris. It was also my first encounter with traditional but unfamiliar Turkish eats, iskembe soup and lahmacun (both which I would be eating plentifully in Istanbul lol). Talking with Ezgi and Baris was plainly put, so much fun. Both are true intellectuals, with deep knowledge on various topics, and held unique opinions that they were wiling to share. It might have been the first 12 Questions where the entirety truly felt like a conversation at a dinner gathering, and I think the “back and forth-ness” or “live” quality can be felt even through the transcribed words below. It was definitely one of the instances where I was laughing from beginning to end. So as you might imagine, yes all the answers were great, full of thought and identity. I would say its worth 10 minutes of your day reading the whole thing, but if I had to choose, they would be the representations (Q.3), the images (Q.5), marriage/children (Q.9), and dreams (Q.12). So Ezgi, Baris see you guys around, and please make it over to Japan and we can do the whisky tour!!!
