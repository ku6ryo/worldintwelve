Pierre Cutellic

Profile and Leo's Take
Age：33　Years in Paris: 10

Pierre was introduced to me by Shu. Pierre is an architect, but also much more. I got to have a nice brunch with Pierre at one of his favourite cafe’s in the North of Paris, a hole-in-the-wall location with that special atmosphere that can only be felt by shops which are loved by the community. Talking with Pierre was an inspiration because he was basically living what I considered the ideal life. He had his own firm, which is centered around architecture, but also is involved with various art and design projects. He also enlightened me on other subjects, such as the startup scene (or lack thereof) in Paris, along with food (and life in general) in various parts of Paris and France. One of the amazing parts of this project is, plainly put, flat out all the new things I learn everyday, and Pierre gave me a plethora of that! And as you might assume, Pierre gave many amazing answers so its difficult to choose recommendations, but if I had to, they would be the representations (Q.3), the future (Q.6), marriage/children (Q.9), and wake up/go to sleep (Q.10). So thanks for everything Pierre, and I’m sure we’ll meet again, somewhere around the world!!
