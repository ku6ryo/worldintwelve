Caio was introduced to me through his mother, Lillian who is colleague professors with Eve and Alex. Caio was absolutely incredible, meeting up with me numerous times during my stay in Rio. He showed me around the Lagoa, went up the Christ Redeemer, and for the 12 Questions, I got to tag along with him and his girlfriend, Fernanda, to check out the Centro area. We visited the the Bank Museum, saw some amazing architecture, and had a memorable time at the cafe with some delicacies. Caio, being a PhD student in Economics, answered the questions with simplicity and precision; I would recommend his important thing (Q.7), his wake up/go to bed (Q.10), and his dreams (Q.12). So Caio, do come to Japan once with Fernanda, and I will give back and show you all around town!!
