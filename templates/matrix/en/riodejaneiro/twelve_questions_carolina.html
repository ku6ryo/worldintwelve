Carolina Z

Profile and Leo's Take
Age：26　Years in Rio de Janeiro： 26 (Egg Yolk Carioca!!)

I met Carolina at Luciana’s birthday party. Carolina, now with her really cool startup that lets foreigners and tourists meet up with local Cariocas (you can check it out below, and use it when you go to Rio), is one of those girls with one of those electrifying personalities. You can almost see the positive energy flowing from her. I recall one moment at the party where she asked how people would act at funerals in Japanese culture (whether they’d bow or not, etc.) so I showed her, and in return, she reenacted a Brazilian funeral---it was absolutely one of the greatest acting jobs I’ve seen, with the excessive bawling, hugging, crying and showing of emotion! Carolina, for her shoot, took me to Aterro do Flamengo, which instantly became my favorite spot in Rio. The beach is beautiful, you can play any sport you want, no tourists only Cariocas—to be honest, its so good, I don’t want to post this so everyone doesn’t find out!! Haha. For Carolina’s answers, I recommend the representation (Q.3), the flag (Q.4) which was eye-opening, the future (Q.6), and her moments (Q.11). So Carolina, thanks again, and I hope your service is going well! Come to Japan, and you can wander with me, the “local”!! Haha

Check out Carolina's startup and service at: www.localwander.com.

Q4
If you were to redsign your country's flag, how would you do it?

A4
Yeah. So! Well… (Thinking)… I think I would do a flag that you could read… that you could see right from both sides. ’Cause our flag has (Laughs) a technical issue. In the middle we have the circle with a wide bend, and the bend is like this (diagonal). So when you see the flag against the sun, the other side makes a shadow, and it turns into an “X.” (Leo bursts into laughter) I don’t like that. It’s also an aesthetic thing.

>>>Wow! Great. I’ve never heard that before. So you want it to be symmetrical.

That works either way, you know. A flag is supposed to be balanced!

>>> Wait…so they actually have two different prints on each side ?

Yeah. They have one print here and another one there. Also, because of the writing.

>>>I see. It’s because from the other side the writing would be from the opposite, so they have to have had the writing from the same side on each side. Interesting!

Yeah, and what I don’t like about it is when you see it against the sun, it’s… it’s really an X in the middle of the flag, so it doesn’t look nice. I don’t like that.

>>>Wow ! That’s really cool. I’d never heard of that.

Yeah. But otherwise I love our flag. I love the colors. I think it’s a really good symbol for Brazilians. Yeah.

Q6
How do you feel about the future? Both yourself and mankind.

A6
For the future? I’m very optimistic. So, I think of a good future. But… for Brazil, the present is not really cheering me up. The present is not that good. But, I think we’re going to have a good future, but we need a lot more time to build it, you know? We need to pass through a lot of challenges in order to, build our citizenship and make a good Brazil. But I believe we’re going to get better as we have been getting better, even with all the issues. And, I think that, for Brazil, a very good thing is the cultural exchange we’re starting to have more now. People are coming here from other countries. They’re teaching us a lot of their culture. And, we’re teaching them. I guess this is something that will help us build a better country. For myself, right now, I think it’s going to be hard to get a very good future here… I think it’s not easy for anyone, especially here in Brazil, but… you know, I like what I do. I like my life. I like to work hard. So, I think it’s gonna’ come. I like the present.

Okay. For the humanity, I also think we’re heading to a much better place. Sometimes people get very scared about some things. And, they always see the bad things, but I see a good world, a world that is getting better on the overall. I don’t watch so much TV, so I think I don’t get so affected by the bad stories. I think that with technology, things are becoming more abundant. So, I see a great future for us all.
