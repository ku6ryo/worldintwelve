Luciana Aguiar and Victor Mascarenhas

Profile and Leo's Take
【Age】 Luciana：28 Victor：28　【Years in Rio de Janeiro】 Luciana：10 Victor：28 (Eggyolk Carioca!)

Luciana and Victor were introduced to me through Pedro, and the first time I met them was at Luciana’s birthday party. It was one of those parties I’ll remember for a long time; I ate the best home made feijoada known to man, and enough brigadeiro to last me a year. Also, I got to meet so many cool people, as seen on previous 12 Qs.

Both Luciana and Victor are designers, and not graphic, on-the-computer designers, but actual product designers. They have their own line called Bolei (you can check out their home page below), and really, their creations are absolutely epic. There is a strong message behind each one, usually revolving around some sort of environmental sustainability and recycling, and the imagination and creativity I witnessed was just off the charts. Victor even gave me one of their “wallets” / money cips, made from scraps used to create surf boards--of course, all environmentally friendly. Wow. That’s all I can say.

As you can imagine, both Luciana and Victor gave great answers. Reading each one is ideal, but if you’re pressed for time, choose the flag (Q.4), the thing (Q.7), the moments (Q.11), and the dreams (Q.12). So Luciana and Victor, we will definitely meet again! I know your products will appear in Tokyo Design Week, so you have to make it over!!

---Check out Victor and Luciana's design product line at: www.boleidesign.com. It's so amazingly cool, you have to take a look!!!
