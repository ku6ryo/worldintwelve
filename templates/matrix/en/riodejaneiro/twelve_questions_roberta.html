Roberta Amorim

Profile and Leo's Take
Age：28　Years in Rio de Janeiro： 11 years (0~6, 23~28)

Roberta (and her boyfriend Pedro) were my Airbnb hosts in Rio, and I have to say I was the luckiest person in all of Zona Sul to be able to stay with these two amazing people. Roberta is just an incredibly thoughtful, kind, considerate, smart, and genuines person with an amazing flowing voice that made her English a pleasure to listen to everyday (I didn't tell her this in person becasue I didn't want to sound like a creep lol). Roberta showed me around PUC, an amazing univesrsity located in the western side of Zona Sul with canals, wild monkeys, and some of the most beautiful women I've ever seen haha. For the questions, Roberta gave simple and thoughful answers reflecting her inner Brazilian, and I would recommend her partner (Q.8), marriage/children (Q.9), wake up/go to bed (Q.10), and her moments (Q.11). Again, thanks for everything Roberta, and I know I will see you soon!!
