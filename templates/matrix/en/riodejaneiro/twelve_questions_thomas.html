Thomás Martin

Profile and Leo's Take
Age：26　Years in Rio de Janeiro： 16

Thomás I met at Luciana's birthday party. He stood out 1, because of his long flowing blonde hair (the one type of hair you don't see much in the extremely diverse Rio), and 2, because he was dancing with his girlfriend (who is amazingly talented, busting out the splits without breaking a sweat!). Thomás is also extremely talented, being the drummer in the metal band Hatefulmurder, which got notice of its South America tour scheduling as I was doing my questions with Thomás! And of course Thomás himself, like most hard-core musicians I've gotten to know, was super kind and friendly, and I had a great time getting to know his background and thoughts. I really wish I could have gone out once more!! For the answers, I recommend the flag (Q.4), the thing (Q.7), his partner (Q.8), and his moments (Q.10). So Thomás, it was great meeting you! See you again, and come to Japan with your band!!

Check out Hatefulmurder at: Hatefulmurder and their facebook page.
