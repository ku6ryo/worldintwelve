Soundscape: San Francisco

As always, everything you hear in the soundscape are actual sounds I recorded while my stay in that city. However, some sounds are modified a little, just so they sound better (getting rid of wind, background noise, etc.) I will give hints below, so try seeing what you can figure out!! (Oh and use of headphones is advised!!)


Some Places:
The Mission, Alta Plaza Park, The Ferry Building, A Private School in Pacific Heights, Berkeley

Some Sounds:
Street Drummers, Street Musicians, People playing their stereos in public, Kids talking to parents, People calling for their dogs, People talking in the park, Sounds on the bus, Stomping
