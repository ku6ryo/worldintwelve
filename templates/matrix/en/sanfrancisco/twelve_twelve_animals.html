12 Stops × 12 Animals

Overview



This is a sub-project where one quantity of 12 is crossed with another quantity of 12 to create representation of that city. In this one, I choose a park I want to visit, get on a train or a bus, go 12 stops, get off, and walk to the rest of the way. While walking, I take photographs of the first 12 animals I see. Hence: "12 Stops × 12 Animals"



Route



I conducted this sub-project after I ate at MyMy's Coffee Shop. I wanted to get to the "Panhandle" section of Golden Gate Park. And here's how it went.



I got off at the 12th stop, so Baker and California.

FInal Thoughts


It was very telling that I met so many dogs during this sub-projects because, while I was living in San Francisco, it was the same of this everyday. People love their dogs in this city, and they love walking them. It's just part of the culture. Juicing, jogging, walking your dog.
