12 Steps × 12 Books

Overview



This is a sub-project where one quantity of 12 is crossed with another quantity of 12 to create representation of that city. In this one, I go to a used bookstore, walk twelve steps from the entrance and find a book case. I choose one book that looks interesting from that case, and proceed onto another one after another 12 steps. I do this until I have found 12 books, then in which I ask either the owner or an employee of the bookstore to choose which one I should buy. Hence: "12 Stops × 12 Animals"



The Bookstore



For San Francisco, I chose "Alexander's Book Company" located downtown near the financial district.



With three floors, its one of the bigger bookstores in San Francisco!
