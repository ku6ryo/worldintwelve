Sauman Ng Agerberg

Profile and Leo's Take
Age：--　Years in Stockholm: 20

Sauman was introduced to me through Enni. My first encounter with Sauman was at a mid-summer dinner in Uppsala at Enni’s flat, where I also got to meet her wonderful husband and beautiful baby. The next time I met Sauman was at her home studio, where I got a full on glam/make up job for a practice shoot. The entire occasion was an interesting experience, especially conducting the interview and getting my hair and face done at the same time! Talking with Sauman was also a learning experience, and everything that she has gone through—from living in Singapore and Dubai, starting and leaving a company that she considered here child—was shocking, exhilarating, and thought provoking. Life, as I’ve witnessed throughout the year talking with so many people, is truly a rollercoaster (and one where you don’t know where you end up!) Sauman gave simple but strong answers (while blowdrying my hair), and if I were to choose some, they would be the important thing (Q.7), marriage/children (Q.9), the moments (Q.11), and the dreams (Q.12). So Sauman, hope to see you soon, and maybe your family can come to Japan!! That would be cool :)

Check out Sauman's portraits at www.smileforthecamera.se. Beautiful people!!
