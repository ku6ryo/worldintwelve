Combining noise, drone, shoegaze, metal and pop, Looprider is a rock band from Tokyo, Japan. Formed and led by Ryotaro Aoki, the band is a revolving door of musicians and genres based on the sound of each particular release.

Website: http://looprider.bandcamp.com, Twitter: @lprdrband, Contact: lprdrband@gmail.com




Leo's Take: Ryotaro and I go back to when I was running my recording studio during my university days. He was in a band then, and he's doing a cool musical project now. I guess we are all changing. This song combines shoegaziness with a beat; its something special where you're body is rocking now and you'll be humming the tune later. This is just the beginning, and I can't wait for more!!
