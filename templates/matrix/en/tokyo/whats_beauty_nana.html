Q1：What is "beauty" to you?
A："Something resemling an aura"
→The natural appeal of something. Something that is original or unique to that person, and comes out when that person is truly being his or herself. When I look at the people around me, the people are trustworthy, that believe in themselves, have confidence, are good human beings--those are the beautiful people. Oh, and also people who have love? (愛がある?) And I believe the world is a beautiful place because love exists! And now I sound like a hippy! Haha

Q2：Where do you think is beautiful in Tokyo?
A："Yoyogi Park!!"
→Why? Because one of the things I'm grateful for while living in Tokyo is that there are the four seasons, and the place where I feel it the most is in Yoyogi Park. In the spring there are the cherry blossoms, in the summer the grass and springs are relaxing, in the fall the autumn leaves are absolutely beautiful, and in the winter, the air is clear and I love walking under the bright blue sky. Its even just fun people watching with everyone having fun, going at their hobbies. When work is stressful or even when I just have free time, I come here to reset and recharge. I feel very fortunate that a place with so much nature exists in the middle of Tokyo.
