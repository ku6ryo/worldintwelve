Sharon Lo

Profile and Leo's Take
Age：22　Years in Toronto： 22

Sharon was introduced to me through both Steven and Myles. Sharon gave me a tour of Hart house, one of the buildings in University of Toronto, which was filled with interesting student art, and interesting looking students running out of the gymnasium wearing tank tops (even though it was approximately -20 degrees Celsius outside). Sharon with her great sense of humour was great fun to talk to, and even made me realize (through her own confession) that she looks eerily similar to one AKB48 member. If you know AKB48, you’ll probably know which one; if you don’t…well I guess google was probably invented for these purposes lol. And of course with her sense of humour came great answers, with my recommendations being the representatives (Q.3), the flag (Q.4), children (Q.9) and wake up/go to sleep (Q.10). So thanks again Sharon, maybe we can go to see a concert together in Akihabara!! Hint hint ;)

Q2
What country do you feel “close” to, other than your own?

A2
It's actually Japan. Yeah. I think the past two years, I've actually met a lot of people who are from Japan. They come here for working holiday. Or… they study here. And in the past three years, I think I've gone three times. Including like an exchange there for two months. And so, I've met a lot of people. And I feel like every time I go, more of me gets left behind. And it's actually really scary. Because I feel like now I have two places that I could potentially call home, especially in the future. And yeah, I have my Tokyo humans and my Toronto humans, as I call them.
