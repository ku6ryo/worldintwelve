Anyway, details are below.

Goal: To have a girl show viewers around the city, to the spots she would love to go on a date. The girl chooses 3 spots for the date, between shops and restaurants. She will be handed 12 dollars/euros/applicable currency for each spot. She can obvously shop for herself, but also must buy a present for the viewers of the site using the money. There really aren't any criteria for choosing the spots, other than places which the girl thinks is unique to the city, and has personal meaning for herself.

There will be 4 girls for each city. 4 girls × 3 spots = 12 places...(I know I'm pushing it)

The winner of the presents will be chosen in the same manner as the presents for the Rules.

So that’s it. Let’s see where these girls will take us!!
